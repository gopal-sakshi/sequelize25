Duplex
- both parties can communicate with each other in both directions


full-Duplex
- both parties can communicate with each other simultaneously.
- telephone

half-Duplex
- both parties can communicate with each other, but not simultaneously
- a walkie-talkie with a push-to-talk button. 
    to speak, push button ===> turns on the transmitter and turns off the receiver
    preventing them from hearing the remote person while talking. 
    to listen, release the button ===> which turns on the receiver and turns off the transmitter.
